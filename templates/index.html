How to Initialize AlertIQ System

  • Step 1: Connect the AlertIQ device to a stable power source.
  • Step 2: Establish network connectivity via Ethernet or secure Wi-Fi.
  • Step 3: Press the 'Initialize System' button on the landing interface.
  • Step 4: Follow the guided on-screen prompts to finalize the setup process.
  • Step 5: Post-initialization, define the primary purpose of your deployment.
  • Step 6: Configure specific operational parameters for chosen mode.
  • Step 7: Confirm settings and monitor system metrics via the live dashboard.
